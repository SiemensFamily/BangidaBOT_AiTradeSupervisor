Ensemble Voting System

Signals are generated by an ensemble of up to 13 strategies. A trade is only placed when:

  • At least 2 strategies agree on direction (Buy or Sell)
  • Weighted average signal strength exceeds ensemble_threshold (default 0.25)
  • Take-profit and stop-loss are taken from the highest-weight contributing strategy
  • The AiTradeSupervisor applies regime-adaptive weighting before consensus

Scalping Strategies

Momentum Breakout

Primary strategy. Detects breakouts with volume confirmation and multi-indicator confluence.

Long Entry

  • Price breaks above 60s high: price > highest_high_60s
  • Volume spike: current_volume / avg_volume_60s ≥ 2.5x
  • RSI not overbought: RSI(14) < 80
  • OBV confirms accumulation + EMA trend: EMA(9) > EMA(21)
  • Multi-timeframe: 5m and 15m trends not Down

Short Entry

Mirror: price below 60s low, RSI > 20, OBV ≤ 0, EMA(9) < EMA(21)

TP: 0.50%  |  SL: 0.25%  |  Trailing: 0.20%

Order Book Imbalance

Microstructure edge from order book depth. PostOnly orders for maker rebates.

  • Spread guard: spread ≤ 2x tick_size
  • Long: imbalance_ratio > 0.30 AND CVD > 0
  • Short: imbalance_ratio < -0.30 AND CVD < 0

TP: 3 ticks  |  SL: 2 ticks

Liquidation Wick Reversal

Detects liquidation cascades and trades the reversal after exhaustion. Low frequency.

  • Liquidation volume spike: liq_volume ≥ 3.0x avg_volume
  • Long (after downward cascade): price_velocity_30s < -1.0%
  • Short (after upward cascade): price_velocity_30s > +1.0%

TP: 0.80%  |  SL: 0.40%

Funding Rate Bias

Supplementary — adds directional bias when funding rates are extreme, does not generate standalone signals.

  • Short bias when funding_rate > 0.05  |  Long bias when funding_rate < -0.05
  • Cross-exchange divergence: |primary - secondary| > 0.025
  • Boosts ensemble strength by +0.10

Swing / Trend-Following Strategies

Donchian Channel Breakout

Classic Turtle Trading entry system. Low win rate (30-40%) but high reward:risk (3:1+). Buy new highs, sell new lows.

Entry Conditions

  • Long: price > highest high of last N bars (default N=20)
  • Short: price < lowest low of last N bars
  • Optional trend filter: price > EMA(200) for longs
  • Strength = 0.80 + magnitude bonus (distance beyond channel / ATR)

TP: ATR x 4.0  |  SL: ATR x 2.0

MA Cross (Moving Average Crossover)

Enter on fast EMA crossing slow EMA with price confirmation. Configurable periods (default 21/50).

Entry Conditions

  • Long: EMA(fast) > EMA(slow) AND price > EMA(fast)
  • Short: EMA(fast) < EMA(slow) AND price < EMA(fast)
  • Min spread between EMAs: (fast - slow) / slow ≥ 0.5%

TP: ATR x 3.0  |  SL: ATR x 1.5

Supertrend Trailing

Follows the Supertrend indicator direction with regime-adaptive strength scaling.

  • Long when supertrend_up = true  |  Short when supertrend_up = false
  • Base strength: 0.82 (long) / 0.78 (short), scaled by regime multiplier
  • Boosted in Volatile regime (1.15x), reduced in Ranging (0.85x)

EMA Pullback

Detects pullback entries in the direction of the prevailing EMA trend.

  • Direction proxy from price_velocity_30s
  • Strength: 0.72 (bullish pullback) / 0.68 (bearish pullback)
  • Filtered by min_pullback_strength config (default 0.60)
  • Reduced in Ranging regime (0.85x)

Mean-Reversion / Order Flow Strategies

Mean Reversion

Fades extreme moves outside Bollinger Bands when RSI confirms oversold/overbought in ranging markets.

Entry Conditions

  • Long: price below BB lower by bb_penetration of band-width AND RSI < 30
  • Short: price above BB upper by bb_penetration of band-width AND RSI > 70
  • ADX filter: ADX(14) < 25 (ranging market only — fails in strong trends)
  • Skipped in Volatile/Extreme regimes

TP: ATR x 1.5  |  SL: ATR x 1.0

CVD Divergence

Uses Cumulative Volume Delta from order flow to detect divergence between price and volume pressure.

  • Long when CVD > 0 (net buying pressure) — strength 0.78
  • Short when CVD < 0 (net selling pressure) — strength 0.65
  • Filtered by min_divergence_strength (default 0.65)

RSI + Fair Value Gap

RSI extreme reversal strategy. Fires when RSI reaches overbought/oversold extremes.

  • Long when RSI(14) < rsi_oversold (default 30) — strength 0.78
  • Short when RSI(14) > rsi_overbought (default 70) — strength 0.75
  • No signal when RSI is in the neutral zone

Volume Profile (placeholder)

Mean reversion at Point of Control / Value Area. Currently uses fixed strength; will be upgraded when POC data is wired from candle history.

Session Retrace (placeholder)

Trades retracements after NY/London session highs and lows. Currently placeholder; will use timestamp-based session detection.

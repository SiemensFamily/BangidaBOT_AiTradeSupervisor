Ensemble Voting System

Signals are generated by an ensemble of 4 strategies. A trade is only placed when:

  • At least 2 strategies agree on direction (Buy or Sell)
  • The weighted average signal strength exceeds the ensemble_threshold (default 0.20)
  • Take-profit and stop-loss are taken from the highest-weight contributing strategy

Regime-Adaptive Weights

Strategy weights automatically adjust based on the detected volatility regime:

Strategy 1: Momentum Breakout (40% weight)

The primary strategy. Detects breakouts with volume confirmation and multi-indicator confluence.

Long Entry Conditions

  • Price breaks above 60-second high: price > highest_high_60s
  • Volume spike: current_volume / avg_volume_60s ≥ 2.5x
  • RSI not overbought: RSI(14) < 80
  • OBV confirms accumulation: OBV ≥ 0
  • EMA trend alignment: EMA(9) > EMA(21)
  • Multi-timeframe: 5m and 15m trends not Down

Short Entry Conditions

  • Price breaks below 60-second low: price < lowest_low_60s
  • Same volume spike, RSI > 20, OBV ≤ 0, EMA(9) < EMA(21)

Exits

Take Profit: 0.50%  |  Stop Loss: 0.25% (2:1 R:R)  |  Trailing Stop: 0.20%

Strategy 2: Order Book Imbalance (25% weight)

Captures short-term microstructure edges using order book depth analysis. Uses PostOnly orders exclusively for maker rebates.

Entry Conditions

  • Spread guard: spread ≤ 2x tick_size
  • Long: imbalance_ratio > 0.30 AND CVD > 0 (buying pressure)
  • Short: imbalance_ratio < -0.30 AND CVD < 0 (selling pressure)

Exits

Take Profit: 3 ticks  |  Stop Loss: 2 ticks

Strategy 3: Liquidation Wick Reversal (20% weight)

Detects liquidation cascades and trades the reversal after exhaustion. High conviction, low frequency (1-3 trades/day).

Entry Conditions

  • Liquidation volume spike: liq_volume ≥ 3.0x avg_volume_60s
  • Long (after downward cascade): price_velocity_30s < -1.0%
  • Short (after upward cascade): price_velocity_30s > +1.0%

Exits

Take Profit: 0.80%  |  Stop Loss: 0.40% (2:1 R:R)

Strategy 4: Funding Rate Bias (15% weight)

Supplementary strategy — does NOT generate standalone signals. Adds directional bias when funding rates are extreme.

  • Short bias when funding_rate > 0.05 (5%)
  • Long bias when funding_rate < -0.05
  • Cross-exchange divergence: |primary - secondary| > 0.025
  • Boosts ensemble signal strength by +0.10
